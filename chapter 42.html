Chapter 42

INT. KING'S HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - LATER

The team is laughing and having a blast playing the game. King and Melany are teamed up, competing against Jay and Jim. Green is commentating, making jokes and teasing his friends.

                                GREEN
(jokingly)
Oh, King's going down!

                                 KING
(laughing)
No way!

                             MELANY
(smiling)
We've got this!

CUT TO:

INT. ROSE'S APARTMENT - DAY

Rose's dad, Mr Harry is packing boxes while Rose sits on the couch, staring at a photo of King on her phone. Tears well up in her eyes.

                                 MR HARRY
(firmly)
Rose, it's time to pack your things. You're leaving for the US soon.

                                    ROSE
(desperate)
But Dad, I love him and I can't leave, I know he's moved on, but I just can't imagine living without him.

                                MR HARRY
(gently)
Rose, you deserve so much better than a guy who's already given his heart to someone else.

Rose buries her face in her hands, sobbing uncontrollably. Her dad walks over and sits beside her.

                                   MR HARRY
(encouragingly)
You're strong, Rose. You'll get through this. And who knows? Maybe you'll find someone even better in the US.

Rose slowly composes herself, wiping away her tears. She looks at King's picture one last time, then deletes it from her phone and starts packing her things, determined to start a new chapter in her life.

CUT TO :

INT. HAEBAK'S OFFICE - DAY

Haebak sits at his desk, surrounded by piles of files and documents. He rubs his temples, looking frazzled.

                                   HAEBAK
(to himself)
How did King do this?

He looks at a file, then tosses it aside.

                                  HAEBAK
(frustrated)
It's different without you, King.

Haebak's assistant enters the room.

                              ASSISTANT
(concerned)
Sir, are you okay? You seem a bit overwhelmed.

                              HAEBAK
(sighing)
Just get me the Johnson report.

The assistant nods and exits the room.

Haebak buries his face in his hands, feeling the weight of his responsibilities.

                               HAEBAK
(to himself)
King, why did you leave me with this mess?

Suddenly, his phone rings. Haebak answers it, listening intently.

                                 HAEBAK
(serious)
Yes, I understand. I'll take care of it.

Haebak hangs up the phone, looking even more stressed.

CUT TO:

INT. KING'S HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - DAY

The team sits in stunned silence, staring at their laptops with a mix of exhaustion and elation. King stands up, a triumphant smile spreading across his face.

                                  KING
(emotionally)
We did it, guys. We actually did it.

The team erupts into cheers and hugs, tears of joy streaming down their faces. Melany leaps into King's arms, embracing him tightly.

                                MELANY
(Proudly)
I'm super happy. We did it in just two weeks.

Jay and Jim high-five each other, grinning from ear to ear.

                                     JIM
(emotionally)
You know, King, I'm just so relieved to see you happy and smiling now.

                                     KING
(smiling)
Thanks, Jim. I couldn't have done it without all of you.

                                   JIM
(sincerely)
No, seriously, King. After everything you went through with Rose and your dad... to see you now, with Melany, working on something you love... it's just amazing.

The team nods in agreement, smiling at King's happiness.

                                   GREEN
(proudly)
MindPal is more than just an app. It's a movement.

King looks around at his team, his eyes shining with pride.

                                     KING
(smiling)
We're not just a team. We're a family.

The team cheers again, raising their hands in a triumphant gesture.

                                     KING
(excitedly)
Alright, now that we've finished the app, it's time to launch it!

                                  MELANY
(smiling)
And share it with the world!

                                    JAY
(jokingly)
Time to change lives!

                                   GREEN
(negatively)
Yeah, if people actually download it...

The team turns to Green, shocked.

                                     JAY
(laughing)
Green!

                                  MELANY
(smiling)
Positive vibes only!

                                     JAY
(jokingly)
Don't be a party pooper!

                                     JIM
(smiling)
Come on, Green!

The team starts chanting Green's name, shouting "Think positive!" and "Don't be negative!"

                                 GREEN
(laughing)
Alright, alright!

                                   KING
(smiling)
That's the spirit!

The team cheers and high-fives each other, excitement and positivity filling the air.
