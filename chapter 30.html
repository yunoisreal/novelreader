My Rude Boss


Chapter 30

CUT TO:

INT. CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

King, stands at the head of the table, his expression cold and detached. Haebak, sits across from him, his eyes fixed on King with a hint of tension.

The conference room is filled with employees from various departments including Melany, Green and Jay, all eager to discuss the new app launch.

                                    KING
(icy tone)
Let's get started. Our new app, Eclipse, is a top priority. I expect everyone to meet their deadlines.

                                HAEBAK
(calmly)
I agree. Our teams need to work together seamlessly to make this launch a success.

Jim, nods in agreement.

                                      JIM
(confidently)
Our development team is on track to meet the deadline. We've also outlined a comprehensive marketing strategy.

The employees begin to discuss and brainstorm ideas, while King and Haebak engage in a subtle, cold stare-down.

Melany speaks up.

                                    MELANY
(curiously)
Sir, how do you envision the launch event?

                                       KING
(briefly)
Leave that to Haebak. He's handling marketing.

                                      HAEBAK
(smiling)
We're planning a launch party with influencers, media coverage, and a live demo.

The employees nod, taking notes.

As the meeting continues, the tension between King and Haebak is palpable, but they maintain their professional composure.

The meeting concludes with a clear plan and assigned tasks.

                                         KING
(Serious)
Let's make Eclipse a success.

                                       HAEBAK
(smiling)
I'll make sure marketing delivers.

The employees begin to file out of the conference room, chatting and laughing.

King and Haebak linger, their eyes locked in a silent, intense stare. Melany Jay and Green stare at them before exiting.

INT. OFFICE HALLWAY - DAY

Green, Melany, and Jay stroll down the hallway, chatting.

                                  GREEN
(dramatically)
Ugh, this new app launch is going to be the death of me! I'm already getting beauty wrinkles.

                               MELANY
(laughing)
Beauty wrinkles?

                                  JAY
(chuckling)
Dude, you're such a girl.

                               GREEN
(smiling)
Hey, someone's got to bring some glamour to this office.

The conversation takes a serious turn.

                                   GREEN
(leaning in)
You guys notice anything weird during the meeting?

                                MELANY
(curious)
Like what?

                                 GREEN
(whispering)
Sir King asked you to switch seats. When it was clear sir Haebak saved a seat for you. I think he's...

                                      JAY
(completing Green's sentence)
Jealous?

                                 MELANY
(laughing)
You guys are ridiculous. Sir King's not jealous.

Just then, they notice King standing behind Melany, his expression unreadable.

                                  GREEN
(urgently)
We're going...

                                     JAY
(quickly)
Yeah, lunch.

                                  MELANY
(confused)
Where are you guys going?

                                  GREEN
(hurriedly)
Just...lunch. We'll catch up later.

Melany looks puzzled as Green and Jay hastily exit.

                                 MELANY
(to herself)
What's wrong with them?

King clears his throat, making Melany jump.

                                 KING
(calmly, still stoic)
Melany, let's have lunch in my office.

Melany hesitates, unsure of what to say.

Suddenly, Haebak's voice echoes from down the hallway.

                             HAEBAK
(into phone)
I'm not available right now...

Haebak glances up, his eyes locking onto King and Melany. A flicker of tension crosses his face before he disappears around the corner.

                                 MELANY
Okay.....

King holds Melany by the hand and they enter his office.

CUT TO:

INT. RESTAURANT - DAY

Haebak sits across from Rose, at a elegant restaurant. Rose sips her coffee, her eyes locked on Haebak.

                                     ROSE
(smirking)
I knew you'd come.

                                      HAEBAK
(curious)
What's this about, Rose?

                                            ROSE
(leaning in)
I know you have feelings for Melany. And I want King back.

                                     HAEBAK
(skeptical)
What makes you think I'd help you?

                                        ROSE
(persistent)
We both want something. You want Melany, and I want King. If we work together, we can both get what we want.

                                    HAEBAK
(firmly)
I'm not interested in playing games, Rose. Melany deserves better than that.

                                    ROSE
(anger flashing in her eyes)
You're making a big mistake, Haebak. You'll never have Melany if you don't play along.
