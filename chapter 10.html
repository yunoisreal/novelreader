CUT TO
Melany is frantically packing her suitcase, throwing clothes and toiletries in haphazardly

                                MELANY
(To herself)
20 minutes? How am I supposed to make it?

                                  MELANY
(Continued)
Why did this have to happen today

INT LIVING ROOM DAY

Melany rushes through the living room grabbing her keys and purse on the way

(To herself)
I hope I didn't forget anything

EXT HOUSE DAY
Melany rushes out of the gate, her suitcase rolling behind her. She looks at her watch and quickens her pace

(to herself)
I have to make it . I'm going to make it

(startled)

Hi ....we are out of workplace so you can call me Jim

(Smiling back)

CUT TO :

                               MELANY
I'm sorry sir I tried.. but....

The camera pans over to king who is still standing there bare chest , he looks back staring at Mel struggling

Almost........
