CUT TO:

INT LIVING ROOM DAY

Rose sits alone in the dimly lit living room, her eyes fixed on the TV screen. King's face fills the screen, a confident smile spreading across his face as he talks about his new company, Emerald. Melany stands beside him, her hand on his arm.

Rose's eyes well up with tears as she watches, her face a picture of sadness and longing. Her lips tremble, and she bites them to hold back a sob.

Just as it seems like she's about to break down, her father walks into the room. He takes one look at Rose's face and his expression softens. He gently takes the remote from the coffee table and switches off the TV.

The room falls silent, except for Rose's quiet sniffles. Her father pats her on the shoulder, a comforting gesture. "It's okay, my dear," he whispers.

Rose nods, still fighting back tears. Her father gives her shoulder one last squeeze before turning and leaving the room. Rose is left alone in the darkness, her heart heavy with sorrow.
