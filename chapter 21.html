Chapter 21

NEXT MORNING

INT. OFFICE - DAY

The office is bustling with employees working at their desks, some chatting quietly. Melany sits at her computer, typing away. She gets up to grab something from the printer and accidentally knocks over a stack of files on her colleague's desk.

                            MELANY
(apologetic)
Oh no, I'm so sorry!

She rushes to help her colleague pick up the files. As they're gathering the papers, Melany's eyes widen in surprise.

MELANY
(to herself)
Is this...? These are the files Mr. King asked me to work on.

She looks up at her colleague.

MELANY
(curious)
Where did you get these files from?

COLLEAGUE
(nonchalantly)
Mr. King asked me to pack them away in the store room before he left for the meeting.

MELANY
(confused)
Why?

COLLEAGUE
(shrugs)
They're old files from years back. I guess he didn't need them anymore.

Melany looks stunned, her eyes fixed on the files.

MELANY
(to herself)
What's going on?

She helps her colleague gather the last of the files and watches as she walks away. Melany's gaze drifts to the glass door of Mr. King's office, where his seat was empty.

Melany's eyes narrow, her expression a mix of confusion and anger.

CUT TO:

Melany walking down the hallway, lost in thought. She's confronted by Green and Jay.

                                GREEN
(concerned)
Hey, Mel, what's wrong? You look like you've seen a ghost.

                                     MELANY
(exasperated)
It's just...I don't understand what's going on.

                                      JAY
(curious)
What do you mean?

                                     MELANY
(frustrated)
Mr. King asked me to work on those files, but then I found out they're old files from years back. And he had them packed away in the store room.

                               GREEN
(skeptical)
That does sound weird. Or maybe he is inlove with you or maybe he wants to spend time with you.

                                    JAY
Green do you have to joke with everything?

                                  GREEN
(Defensively)
What? I'm just saying

                                       JAY
(Sighs)
Mel why don't you confront him when he is back from the meeting.

Melany looks confused.

LATER
INT OFFICE BUILDING DAY

Rose steps into the elevator with smiles all over her face ready to meet king.
As the door closes,she is joined by two colleagues Who are chatting animatedly.

                                COLLEAGUE 1
(Whispering conspirationally)
Hey did you hear ?

                                COLLEAGUE 2
(Curious)
What tell me.

                                 COLLEAGUE 2
(Whispering)
Melany spent the whole day in sir king's office yesterday. And they both ate lunch together and that's not all they left together too.

                                COLLEAGUE 1
(Raising an eyebrow)
That's not surprising. Since Melany became his personal assistant, sir king has really changed a lot and I think I like the new side of him.

Rose's eyes widen as she listens, her expression darkening.

                                    COLLEAGUE 2
(Whispering)
Hey but don't you realize sir Haebak is also inlove with Melany?

                               COLLEAGUE 1
(Whispering)
Yeah, I noticed but am rooting for sir king and Melany.

                                    COLLEAGUE 2
Yeah me too. "Go kimel!"

As the two colleagues laughed without noticing Rose's presence, Rose twist in anger her fists clenched she remembers the day she followed king and saw her dropping of Melany at home.

                                     ROSE
(To herself)
Can it be? Is it the same woman?

The elevator stops, and the door opens the two colleagues step out still chatting.

Rose follows, her eyes blazing with anger and hurt.
Rose steps out of the elevator, still seething with anger, she spots a colleague walking by.

                                  ROSE
(Venomously)
Excuse me, who is Melany?

The colleague hesitates then points Down the hallway.

                               COLLEAGUE
(Nervously)
That's her. But ma'am sir king is not around.

Rose eyes follows the colleague's gaze and spots Melany walking to her seat.

Rose's face twist with anger as she realizes it is the woman king dropped home yesterday. She storms over to Melany

                                   ROSE
(Sneering)
You think you're so special don't you? Stealing kings attention from me?

Melany looks taken aback by rose's venom

                                    MELANY
(Firmly)
I don't know what you are talking about.

                                    ROSE
(Cruelly)
Don't play dumb with me. I know all about you and king.

Green Jay Jim and the other colleagues start to gather around looking at the scene.

                                       JAY
(Defensively)
Don't talk to my friend like that. She has no idea of what you are saying.
