INT. MELANY'S OLD ROOM - DAY

Mrs. Wang slowly walks into Melany's old room, her eyes scanning the space as if searching for a ghost. She sits on the bed, her hands trembling as she reaches for a picture frame on the nightstand.

The frame holds a family photo, taken when Melany's father was still alive. Mrs. Wang's eyes well up with tears as she gazes at the smiling faces.

She weeps, hugging the frame tightly, as if holding onto the memories. Her body shakes with sobs, and she whispers Melany's name.

Mrs. Wang's hands wander over the bed, touching the familiar contours of Melany's old pillow. She picks up a blouse Melany left behind, holding it to her face and inhaling deeply.

Suddenly, Cheri enters the room, her expression softening as she sees her mother's tears.

The atmosphere is heavy with sorrow, regret, and longing. The camera lingers on the family photo, a poignant reminder of what's been lost.
