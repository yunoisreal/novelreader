Chapter 27

Melany hesitantly tries on the dress, looking stunning out of place. King notices her discomfort but keeps mute about it.

After a while they exit the shop with the sales associate carrying the shopping bags.

CUT TO :

INT RESTAURANT NIGHT
King and Melany sit at an elegant restaurant. A waiter approaches them.

                               WAITER
(Smiling)
Welcome! May I take your order?

                                 KING
(Confidently)
We'll have the caviar, the lobster thermidor, and a bottle of your finest champagne.

                                  WAITER
(Smiling)
Okay will be back in a few minutes

                                     MELANY
(Uncomfortably)
Uh... Sir ..

                                      KING
(Firmly)
No buts you have to eat to stay healthy.

Few moments later, Melany looks overwhelmed as the waiter presents her with plates of caviar.

                                  MELANY
(Nervously)
I...I don't know how to eat this sir..

                                  KING
(Coldly)
Just try it....

Melany takes a small bite clearly uncomfortable.

CUT TO :

King sits in his study a laptop open Infront of him, a video call with Jim.

                                   KING
(Coldly)
I just told Melany she is my girlfriend

                                   JIM
(Via video call, surprised)
Huh?!  That's huge but shouldn't you have asked her how she feels about it instead of just telling her

                                 KING
(Unfazed)
I don't need her opinion I've made up my mind.

                                   JIM
(Via video call, concerned)
King you can't just claim someone as your girlfriend without their consent that's not how relationship works.

                                 KING
(Skeptical)
You know am not good at this like you are.

                                  JIM
(Via video call, teasingly)
Did you just compliment me?

                              KING
(Serious)
I'm serious here Jim. (Pauses) I took her shopping and to dinner today but she seemed...... uncomfortable.

                             JIM
(Via video call, amused)
That's because she isn't like Rose. She's not used to your luxurious lifestyle king. You can't expect her to adjust overnight.

                             KING
(Frowning)
What do you mean?

                                JIM
(Via video call, practical)
Melany didn't grow up with wealth and privilege. She's not accustomed to designer clothes and fine dining. She's just a simple sweet girl.

                                 KING
(Serious)
What do you think I should do.

                                 JIM
(Via video call, teasingly)
Just search on " how to smile"

                                KING
(Serious)
Jim I'm serious here

                               JIM
(Via video call)
Well, I already told you I'm not spoon feeding you. (Pauses) Listen king just be yourself. Take Melany somewhere she'll enjoy and feel comfortable. Take her out on a walk or something.

(Via video call, teasingly)
And smile often you are literally scaring her with your stone face.

(Stern)
You are irritating me bye.

Jim laughs as king ends the call and sighs he stares blankly at the walls lost in thoughts.

FADE TO BLACK

CUT TO :
INT - KINGS MANSION - HALL - EVENING
Melany sits on a couch, looking relaxed, king enters dressed in a stylish outfit.

                                  KING
(Coldly)
Get dresses we're going for a walk.

Melany looks surprised but nods, a hint of excitement in her eyes.

CUT TO :
EXT - QUITE STREET - EVENING
King and Melany walk side by side in silence the street is dimly lit, and the air is chilly. King removes his jacket and wraps it around melany's shoulder.

                                     MELANY
(Smiling slightly)
Thank you.

King's eyes flicker with a hint of warmth but his expression remains stoic.

As they continue walking Melany accidentally trips on a loose paving stone, king quickly catches her his hands wrapping around her waist.

                                MELANY
(Laughing)
I'm sorry

                                KING
(Softly)
You're okay.

For a moment they're locked in a gentle embrace their faces inches apart. Then king releases her, and they continue walking.

  CUT TO :
EXT - SMALL RESTAURANT - EVENING
They reach a small, cozy restaurant with a vintage sign reading "Mama's kitchen" the exterior looks rustic, with wooden accents and colorful flowers.

                                     MELANY
(Looking at the restaurant)
Wow!

                                       KING
(Noticing her interest)
Let's go

Inside, the restaurant is warm and inviting, with the aroma of homemade cooking filling the air. King looks around, taking in the unfamiliar surrounding.

                                       MELANY
(Smiling)
What do you think ?
