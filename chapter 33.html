INT. CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

A hint of a smile plays on his lips as he remains silent, observing the scene.

As the discussion continues, King suddenly speaks up, his voice commanding attention.

                                 KING
(confidently)
Miss King, are you done with the notes?

The room falls silent, with all eyes on Melany. She looks up, surprised and shy, her face flushing.

                          MELANY
(nervously)
Y-yes, sir.

Haebak looks confused and jealous, his eyes narrowing slightly. Jay and green exchange a happy glance. While Jim stare at king smiling.

                                JIM
(amused)
(smirking at King, shaking his head)
Woah, my friend is something.

The other employees exchange knowing glances, murmuring and whispering to each other. Some smile, sensing the underlying tension.

Melany's eyes dart to King, her expression a mix of surprise and shyness.

Haebak's gaze lingers on Melany, his eyes filled with a hint of jealousy.

King's expression remains calm, but his eyes sparkle with a hint of amusement.

CUT TO :

The meeting is over and everyone files out, except king, who remains seated going through some files.

CUT TO :

INT - OFFICE - DAY
Melany quickly walks out of the conference room, looking flustered Jim follows her.

                                   JIMIN
(Smiling)
Hey Melany.

                                    MELANY
(Smiling)
Hi sir Jim.

                                JIMIN(CONT'D)
(Serious tone)
I know king might seem to have difficulty in expressing his feelings sometimes. I just want you to know king loves you very much.(Pauses) It's been ages I've seen king like this. He now smiles and tries to express his feelings. Please take care of him.

Melany nods and smiles.

                                   JIMIN
(Smiling)
Thank you.

Jim heads back to the conference room leaving Melany looking thoughtful.

Melany walks back to her desk looking a bit dazed. Green and Jay walks up to her teasing her, trying to get a rise out of her .

                                      GREEN
(Teasingly)
Wow! Sir king is so possessive.

                                     JAY
(Laughing)
Yeah, " miss king" I love the name.

Melany playfully rolls her eyes and shoos them away.

                                   MELANY
(Laughing)
Go away you two! Jay, go have lunch with sir Jim.

                                      JAY
(Smiling)
Okay okay " miss king"

                                     GREEN
(Laughing)
Bye " miss king".

Green and Jay walk away, still teasing Melany.

CUT TO :

INT -  CONFERENCE -  ROOM - DAY
Jim enters the conference room, where king is still seated.

                                      JIMIN
(Smiling)
Hey king. What was that earlier? Calling Melany "miss king" in the middle of a meeting

                                    KING
(Smirking)
Just a little something to keep things interesting.

                                    JIMIN
(Laughs)
I knew it! You were jealous!

King raises an eyebrow, but Jim just winks and walks out.

CUT TO :

INT - CAFETERIA - DAY
Haebak finds Melany sitting alone at a table. He takes a deep breath and sits down beside her.

                                      HAEBAK
(Nervously)
Melany, can I talk to you?

                                         MELANY
(Smiling)
Of course, Haebak.

                             HAEBAK (CONT'D)
(Taking a deep breath)
I just wanted to say....... Melany I have feelings for you. It may seem sudden but I think you are an amazing person and I'd love the chance to get to know you better.

Melany looks startled. Before she could utter a word Haebak continues.

                                 HAEBAK (CONT'D)
(Smiling)
Think about it okay, I'll give you all the time you need.

Haebak gets up and walks away leaving Melany looking stunned and confused.

CUT TO :

Melany is still seated at the cafeteria, looking confused. Jay and Green approach her, concerned.

                                      JAY
(curious)
