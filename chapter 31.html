EXT. RESTAURANT - DAY

Haebak approaches his car, reaching for the door handle. As he's about to enter, he sees Melany and King walking out of the nearby ice cream parlor.

King's eyes crinkle at the corners as he smiles, feeding Melany a bite of ice cream. Melany's cheeks flush, her eyes sparkling with delight.

Haebak's hand freezes on the door handle, his eyes locked onto the pair. A pang shoots through his chest, his mind reeling with questions.

What's going on between them?

Haebak's gaze lingers on King's smiling face, his eyes warm with affection. Melany's shy smile and sparkling eyes only add to Haebak's turmoil.

                            HAEBAK
(whispering to himself, his voice laced with pain)
No... it can't be.

Haebak's eyes remain fixed on the pair, his heart heavy with a mix of emotions.

CUT TO :

FADE IN:

INT. ROSE'S APARTMENT - NIGHT

Rose sits alone in her dimly lit apartment, sipping whiskey from a glass. Her eyes gleam with a mixture of sorrow, anger, and jealousy.

ROSE
(whispering to herself, her voice laced with venom)
I won't let him go. King must be mine and no one else's.

ROSE (CONT'D)

ROSE (CONT'D)

ROSE (CONT'D)

ROSE (CONT'D)

CUT TO:

King and Melany are in their bedroom, getting ready for bed. King's expression is neutral, but his eyes betray a hint of possessiveness.

KING
(firmly)
Come here, Miss King.

Melany looks up at King, her eyes wide with a mix of shyness and curiosity. She hesitantly walks over to him.

KING
(authoritative)
Cuddle with me.

Melany's face flushes, but she nods slightly. King opens his arms, and Melany snuggles into his chest, her face buried in his shoulder.

King wraps his arms around her, holding her close. His expression remains neutral, but his grip on her is possessive.

                                     KING
(Softly)
Do you like your new name? You shouldn't call me sir call me by name because I'm your boyfriend?okay?
Melany slightly smiles.

                                    MELANY
(Softly)
Okay.... King                                 

As they settle in, they lie in silent for a while. King whispers in Melany's ear:

                             KING
(whispering, emotionless)
I love you, Miss King.

Melany's heart skips a beat, but she doesn't respond. She's already asleep, exhausted from the day.

King's expression doesn't change, but he holds Melany closer, his eyes closing as he drifts off to sleep.
