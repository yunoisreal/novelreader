                                   JAY
(beaming)
Isn't it beautiful?

                                 MELANY
(admiring)
Oh, Jay, it's stunning!

                                    GREEN
(teasingly)
You're finally off the market.

                                     JAY
(laughing)
Shut up, Green!

The three friends chat and giggle, discussing wedding plans and Jay's excitement for her future with Jim.

(curiously)

                                       JAY

                                       JAY

CUT TO :

Melany approaches King's desk, a playful glint in her eye.

                                     MELANY
(persistently)
King, please can I ask you for a favor?

                                     KING
(not looking up from his file)
What is it? Miss king.

                                   MELANY
(batting her eyelashes)
I've been craving donuts all day. Just one won't hurt, will it?

King doesn't look up, his expression unchanging.

                                         KING
(still focused on his file)
No.

Melany makes a cute, pouting face, her eyes wide and her lips pursed.

                                      MELANY
(whining)
But why not? It's just one little donut.

King still doesn't look up, but a hint of a smile plays on his lips.

                                        KING
(still expressionless)
Because you know I don't want you eating unhealthy foods.

Melany walks around the desk and stands beside King, her hand on his shoulder.

                                     MELANY
(persistently)
Just this once? For me?

King finally looks up, his eyes meeting Melany's.

                                      KING
(sternly)
Miss king, stop trying to persuade me.

Melany leans in close, her voice barely above a whisper.

                                      MELANY
(seductively)
But I love donuts. And I love you.

King's expression falters for a moment, and he looks away.

                                     KING
(quietly)
Fine. One donut.

Melany squeals in delight and throws her arms around King's neck.

                                     MELANY
(excitedly)
Thank you, King!

King's expression softens, and he wraps his arms around Melany's waist.

                                      KING
(smiling slightly)
Just don't tell anyone I'm spoiling you.

Melany grins mischievously and leans in to kiss King's cheek.

FADE TO BLACK.

CUT TO :
INT - OFFICE - DAY

Sometime later Haebak walks into King's office, looking for him. He calls out King's name, but there's no response.

Haebak pulls out his phone and dials King's number. As he waits for King to pick up, he walks closer to King's desk.

His eyes fall on King's phone, lying face-up on the desk. Haebak's gaze lingers on the screen, and his eyes narrow as he sees Melany's photo as King's wallpaper.

Haebak's expression darkens, his anger and resentment towards King simmering just below the surface.

Just then, King walks into the office, his cold, calculating gaze meeting Haebak's angry stare.

                                KING
(coldly)
What are you doing here, Haebak?

                              HAEBAK
(anger simmering)
I was looking for you. But I found something interesting instead.

Haebak gestures to King's phone, his eyes never leaving King's face.

                              HAEBAK
(accusingly)
Why do you have Melany's picture as your wallpaper?

                                     KING
(unfazed)
Can't I use my girlfriends picture for my wallpaper?

Haebak's eyes widen in shock, his anger boiling over.

                                   HAEBAK
(furiously)
You're dating Melany?! Behind my back?!

                                       KING
(smirking)
I don't need your permission, Haebak.

                                     HAEBAK
(anger and hurt)
I'm inlove with Melany


                                      HAEBAK
(taking a deep breath)
I like her, King. I've had feelings for her for a long time.

                                     KING
(Furious)
You can't be inlove with her. She's mine.
