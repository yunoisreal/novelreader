Chapter 36

Melany walks in to find king and Haebak in a tense standoff. Haebak face is red with anger, while king's expression is cold and distant.

                               HAEBAK
(Furiously)
Mel is it true? Are you dating king?

Melany takes a step back, nervously glancing at king.

                                    MELANY
(Stammering)
I ... I yes.

Haebak eyes widen in shock and hurt.

                                   MELANY
(Softly)
Am sorry Haebak.....

Melany looks down guiltily.

Just then Mr. Lee angrily bursts in, his face red with rage with Jim following behind looking worried.

                                     MR LEE
(Furiously)
What's going on here?

Melany bows respectfully, her head lowered.

                                      MR LEE
(Outraged)
King! is what I'm hearing true? Are you dating your secretary.

He turns to Melany.

                                  MR LEE
(Outraged)
And you! You are the one causing all this trouble.

                                    KING
(Defensively)
Dad, don't talk to my girlfriend like that. I fell inlove with her first and she has nothing to do with this.

                                   MR LEE
(Angrily)
What is wrong with you? You're supposed to be with Rose! And plan your wedding.

King stands tall his eyes flashing with defiance.

                          MR LEE (CONT'D)
You are ruining everything king. Rose's father has a significant share in our company and if you don't marry her, he'll pull out his investment and destroy our business.

                                   KING
(Coldly)
I don't care about the company or Rose! I will not marry her!.

                                     MR LEE
(Enraged)
You ungrateful child! You will do as I say or you face the consequences.

                                   KING
(Emotionally)
Why don't you ever care about my happiness? It's either the company or Haebak you care about. Are you really my dad? If it's really important to you why don't you make your darling son Haebak marry her. Why do you want to sacrifice my happiness.

Melany eyes well up with tears as king continues.
While jim stands there looking worried.

                               KING (CONT'D)
I love Melany because she gave me the things you couldn't give me as a father. She cares for me, she loves me , she makes me happy and I won't allow you or anyone take her away from me!

Mr Lee takes a step closer, his voice dripping with venom.

                                     MR LEE
(Sneering)
You're just like your mother - weak, selfish and ungrateful.

King eyes blaze with anger.

                                     KING
( Furiously)
I won't allow you talk about my mother like that! You never cared about her, or anyone else but yourself.

King voice rises, his words pouring out in a torrent of anger.

                                        KING
(Bitterly)
You're a heartless, selfish man who only cares about power and money!.

Mr Lee slaps king across the face, and Haebak rushes to intervene.

                                      HAEBAK
(Protective)
Dad, stop.

                                        MELANY
(Horrified)
No!

Melany's eyes widen in shock, her face pale with concern. She takes a step forward, as if to intervene. Jim's face twist in anger, his fists clenched at his sides.

The employees outside the office including Jay and Green peek through the glass Walls trying to get a glimpse ot the argument.

Tears well up in kings eyes as he turns to his father.

                                   KING
(Venomously)
You're dead to me.

King turns, grabs Melany by the hand and walks out, leaving Jim worried and his father and haebak in the office. The employees stare at king with worried faces as he walks out of the office building holding melany's hands.

CUT TO :
INT -  CAR -  DAY

King drives in silence, his eyes fixed on the road. Melany sits beside him, her eyes widen with concern.

CUT TO :

INT - KING'S MANSION - DAY

King and Melany enters the mansion, the door closing behind them. Kings face crumples, as he burst into tears.

                                    MELANY
(Soothingly)
King, I'm here.

King wraps his arms around Melany, holding her tightly as he cries. Melany rubs his back comforting him.

The scene fades to black as they stand there hugging.

CUT TO :

INT. HAEBAK'S APARTMENT - NIGHT

Haebak sits on his couch, nursing a glass of whisky. He stares blankly into the distance, his eyes filled with a deep sadness.

As he takes a sip of his drink, his mind begins to wander back to Melany.

CUT TO:

A MONTAGE OF HAEBAK'S MEMORIES WITH MELANY

We see flashes of Haebak and Melany laughing together, sharing moments of tenderness, and exchanging sweet glances.

Haebak's eyes well up with tears as he remembers the way Melany used to smile at him.
