My Rude Boss


Chapter 13

                                  KING
(Disapprovingly)
What's your point Melany

                                MELANY
(Persistently)
Can we please go , just for a little while, I promise I would make up time for tomorrow.

                                 MELANY
(Skeptically)
I don't think it's a good Idea........ Tomorrow is Monday and We've got lot of work to do.

                                      MELANY
(Pleadingly)
Please sir, please please it will be fun

King sighs and looks at Melany for a moment. His expression softens but he quickly regains his composure.

                                   KING
(Firmly)
Fine, but just for an hour

                                     MELANY
(Triumphantly)
Yes! Thank you sir .

CUT TO :

INT AMUSEMENT PARK DAY
King and Melany enters the amusement park . King looks out of place his expression stern and yielding seeing a lot of people

                                MELANY
(Excitedly)
Wow sir, let's go on the roller coaster.

                                 KING
(Disapprovingly)
I don't think that's a good idea

                                  MELANY
C'mon sir it will be fun.

King reluctantly agreed and they get in line for the roller coaster as they are waiting Melany tries to get king to smile but he remains stone face.

                                    MELANY
(Teasingly)
You are not going to scream on the roller coaster. Are you sir?

                                       KING
(Skeptically)
I highly doubt it

They get on the roller coaster and as they crest the first hill Melany screams with delight. King remains silence his expression unchanging.

                                       MELANY
(Laughing)
That was amazing didn't you have fun?

                                           KING
(Disapprovingly)
It was......... Tolerable

Melany laughs and playfully roll her eyes . King stares at her and slightly smiles. They exit the roller coaster and continue through the park,with Melany trying to crack a smile on kings face.

                                       MELANY
(Excitedly)
Oh sir, let's play some game!

                                        KING
(Disapprovingly)
I don't think that is necessary we have a long way to go and it's getting late.

                                   MELANY
(Persistently)
Come on sir it will be fun please just one more game, please !

King reluctantly agreed. Melany grabs king by the hand and pulls him king stare at her. They play a few games including balloon darts and ring toss despite his initial reluctant . King start to show a hint of interest, but he still refuses to smile

King looks down at his phone, which is still ringing he sighs and turns it off.

                                  MELANY
(Teasingly)
You are actually good at this sir.

                                      KING
(Disapprovingly)
Is still a matter of logic and skills

As they are playing Melany notices a photo booth nearby.

                                     MELANY
(Excitedly)
Oh! Sir, let's take some photos

                                         KING
(Disapprovingly)
I don't think that is necessary

                                        MELANY
(Persistently)
Please just this one.

King reluctantly agrees, and they take a few photos together as they are posing. King looks at Melany cutely posing, he smiles but he remains stone face

                                    MELANY
(Laughing)
You are not going to smile for the camera sir king

                                        KING
(Disapprovingly)
I don't see the point.

The photos booth prints out their photos and Melany looks at them laughing.

                                        MELANY
(Teasingly)
You look so serious in this photo

                                            KING
(Disapprovingly)
You think so?

Melany smiles at king and puts one of the photos into kings pocket and puts the other one in her bag.

As they are leaving the park, she turns to king

                                      KING
(Smiling)
You are not that bad as you portray. You just need to smile a lot thank you for today sir king I had alot of fun.

The scene fades to black as they exit the park.

CUT TO :
EXT HOUSE NIGHT

Kings car halts in front of Melany house, they sit in silence for a moment.

                                       MELANY
(breaking the silence)
Sir this is where I live

                                          KING
(Sternly)
Hm ..... Okay get some rest.

                                           MELANY
(Smiling)
Okay sir you too, bye!

  As Melany steps out of the car king stares at her as if to not let her go. She shuts the door behind her

                                       MELANY
(Smiling)
Good bye sir, drive safe. see you tomorrow

As they part ways king couldn't help but smile

Melany is about to enter when her phone rings

                              MELANY
(Into the phone)
Hey jay........
