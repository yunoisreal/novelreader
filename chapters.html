My Rude Boss

By Joan Selikumah

About the author

This story follows a young woman,Melany who has been struggling with the great emotional and physical toll of being blamed for her father's death since childhood. Her life takes an unexpected turn when she secures a job as a secretary to a well-to-do,ill-tempered boss. As they spend more time together,he gradually lowers his guard, and a romance blossoms between them.

However,their newfound love is complicated by the emergence of his brother,who also develops feelings for her,creating a tense love triangle. The narrative explores how this complex romantic instance unfolds and how Melany confronts her problematic family issues
