Chapter 21

                                    KING
(to the phone)
Order  some food for us.

                           MELANY
Sir, I can just go grab something myself.

                              KING
(Firmly)
No, I want you to stay here

Melany looks confused at king as he burries his face in his computer still stern.

Haebak arrives at Melany desk and is looking around. He spots Jay and Green going for lunch break

                                    HAEBAK
(To Jay and Green)
Where is Melany?

                                     JAY
She's been in sir king's office since morning.

                                   GREEN
(Adds)
Yeah, looks like she won't be coming out for lunch.

                                   HAEBAK
(Concerned)
She needs to eat.

Haebak looks concerned and approaches kings office.

                                    JIM
(Blocking Haebak's way)
I'm afraid they're busy Haebak.

                                      HAEBAK
(Faustrated)
It's break and she needs to eat.

                                         JIM
(Calmly)
King has already ordered food for them.

Haebak looks suspicious but Jim holds his ground

                               JIM (CONT'D)
Why don't you join us for lunch instead?

Haebak hesitates then nods.

CUT TO :

INT KINGS OFFICE DAY
King and Melany are eating on his table in kings office. Kings expression remains stoic but his eyes betray a hint of warmth.

                                   KING
(To Melany)
Eat

                                   MELANY
(slightly smiling)
Thank you, sir.

As they eat, king's gaze lingers on Melany who is enjoying her meal, but he quickly looks away, attempting to maintain his stoic demeanor.

As they continue eating, king notices a food crumb Melany's lip. He hesitates for a moment, then reaches out to wipe it away.

                                     KING
(Gruffly)
You have something on your lip.

Melany's eye meet king, and for a fleeting moment, they share a connection.

                                 MELANY
(Softly)
Thank you, sir.

They continue to eat. King's expression remains impassive, but his eyes hold a glimmer of emotion.

  LATER - EVENING
As the sun dips below the horizon, the employees start exiting the office building biding each other goodbye. Haebak comes back to Melany desk holding the bouquet of flowers and the gift bag. His expression disappointed. He walks away looking all gloomy..

Melany heads slowly drops as she falls asleep. Kings expression softens as he watches her a pang of tenderness in his chest .
King gently shakes Melany awake

                                KING
Melany, it's time to go home.

Melany wakes up, distorted

                                   MELANY
(Groggily)
Huh?

                                     KING
Get up I will give you a ride home

Melany nods still sleepy. She packs her things and headed outside following king from behind

EXT KING'S CAR
King and Melany walks towards the car parked outside the office building.

                                    KING
(Opening the door for Melany)
Get in.

Melany nods and slides into the passenger seat.

EXT ACROSS THE STREET
Rose watches from her car parked across the street, her eyes narrow as she sees king and Melany.

                                   ROSE
(To herself)
Who is she?

Rose expression darkens as she starts the engine and follows kings car from a distance.

EXT MELANY HOUSE NIGHT
King drops Melany off, his eyes lingering on her face

                                    MELANY
(Thanking him)
Thank you sir.. goodnight

                                     KING
(still stern)
Okay.

As Melany steps out of the car, king can't help but feel a spark.

Rose watches from her car, her eyes flashing with anger and jealousy.

KING

Huh? What did you mean then?
