My Rude Boss


Chapter 12

In thought as she gazes out at the ocean

                                    JIMIN
(Softly)
             Jay

  Jay turns to see Jimin standing behind her, his eyes fixed on hers. For a moment, they just stare at each other.

                                 JAY
(curious)
Jim what are you doing here?

                                     JIMIN
(Calmly)
I could ask you the same thing.

   Jay shrugs and Jimin walks closer standing beside her

                                     JIMIN
(Looking out at the ocean)
It is beautiful, isn't it ?

                                     JAY
(Softly)
           Yeah

  They stand in silence for a moment watching the waves.

                                     JIMIN
(Breaking the silence)
Have you heard from melany?

                                      JAY
Yes we just spoke ..... I miss her

                                      JIMIN
Seems like you guys are really close

                                       JAY
(Smiling)
Yeah we are inseparable, I have been with her since highschool

Jimin smiles, stares at her in silence as the cool breeze blows her hair behind her back.

                                       JIMIN
( Breaking the silence)
Jay, I have something to tell you.

                                     JAY
(Curious)
What is it ?

                                    JIMIN
(Hesitant)
I...I ...I think I have feelings for you

Jay eyes widened in shock, her mouth dropping open. She felt like she had been punched in the gutt ,her breath knocked out of her .

                                    JIMIN
(Continued)
At first, I thought it was just professional admiration, but the more am around you, the more I realize how I always want you by my side

Jay remains silent . Her eyes fixed on Jimin

                                  JIMIN
Jay , please say something

                                  JAY
(Still in shock)
I...I....I don't know what to say

                                   JIMIN
Take all the time you need, I just wanted to be honest with you.

The only sound is the crashing of the waves as jay continues to stare at Jimin. Her expression unreadable.

CUT TO :
  
INT HOTEL ROOM NIGHT

Melany emerges from the bathroom, as she tries to dry her hair with a towel, king watches her his expression unreadable
Suddenly a knock bursts on the door. King opens the door and collects the package he puts it on the table and brings out it's content. Turns out it is food

                                      MELANY
Oh, wow! Pad Thai!!

                                        KING
(Still cold)
Eat it

They eat in silence, the only sound being the clinking of the utensils.

After dinner, king gestures to the sofa

                                    KING
(Brusquely)
You sleep there

                                     MELANY
(Thinking to herself)
Huh! ........ He is not even a gentle man. Do you always have to be this rude, hm rude boss

King throws a pillow and a blanket at her

                                       KING
(Sternly)
Sleep

Melany settles down into the sofa, watching videos on her phone. King turns off the light and gets into bed

INT HOTEL ROOM LATER
Melany is still watching videos

                                    KING
(Irritated)
You are noisy

Melany lowers the volume, but king tells her to switch off the phone

                                      KING
(Irritated)
Switch off the phone! ...... We have a serious business to deal with tomorrow so sleep.

                                    MELANY
(To herself)
Annoying

INT HOTEL ROOM LATER

Melany falls asleep but king can't. He gets over to Melany on the sofa . He gently covers her with a blanket and stares at her.

FADE OUT :

INT HOTEL ROOM MORNING

Melany wakes up to the sound of water sprinkling on her face. She opens her eyes to see king standing in front of her pouring water from his hair onto hers

                                      KING
(Sternly)
Are you going to sleep all day?

                                        MELANY
(Groggily)
What time is it ?

                                          KING
(Still stern)
Seven ... I'm not going to loose this deal because of you so get up and let's go.

CUT TO :

INT CONFERENCE ROOM DAY

The conference meeting has just ended. King and Melany are packing up their things.

                                      KING
(Brusquely)
Alright let's get going. We've got a long way ahead of us.

                                  MELANY
(Yawning)
Okay sir.

King phone rings, and he looks down to see the caller ID it is Rose. His expression changes, and he hesitates for a moment before ignoring the call.

They exit the conference room and head to the parking garage

CUT TO :
INT CAR  DAY

King and Melany are driving home they stopped at a red light, and Melany looks out of the window and sees an amusement park

                                   MELANY
(Excitedly)
Oh, wow sir an amusement park .
